Tips

Introduction

Write a short introduction about yourself that highlights your career progress, skills, and achievements.

Education

Describe your education by listing colleges and universities you have attended; outstanding achievements or extracurricular activities you participate in during your studies.

Experience

List your work experience (positions you have held, your responsibilities in that position). For example:

Job position - Company name - Time

- Job description:

- Achievements:

Skills

List the skills related to the job you are applying for, including: hard skills, soft skills, personality ..

Awards

List the awards you have won (can list: award name, achievement, organizational unit, details of award content, ...). For example:

01-2021: Award name

02-2021: Award name

Social Media

Links to personal websites or work-related social media accounts such as Linkedin can be added. Do not fill in personal social networking accounts, containing private information not related to your work.
